Guduchi (Tinospora cordifolia)

Description: Guduchi, also known as Giloy, is a powerful adaptogenic herb widely used in Ayurveda to enhance immunity and overall well-being.

Medicinal Uses

  • Boosts immunity and fights infections
  • Supports liver function and detoxification
  • Reduces stress and improves cognitive function
  • Regulates blood sugar levels
  • Has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties
  • Improves digestion and supports gut health

Habitat

Guduchi is native to India and grows well in tropical and subtropical climates.

Part Used

Stem, leaves, and roots.

Where It Is Found in India

Guduchi is widely found across India, particularly in Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu. It grows in forests, hedges, and gardens.

Preparation Methods

  • Consumed as a decoction for immune support
  • Used in Ayurvedic formulations for detoxification
  • Extracted into herbal tonics and capsules
  • Boiled in water and consumed as herbal tea
  • Applied as a paste for skin health
