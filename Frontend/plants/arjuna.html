Arjuna (Terminalia arjuna)

Description: Arjuna is a medicinal tree known for its strong cardioprotective properties. It has been widely used in Ayurveda to support heart health and overall well-being.

Medicinal Uses

  • Supports heart health and regulates blood pressure
  • Reduces cholesterol and strengthens blood vessels
  • Has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties
  • Enhances liver function and detoxification
  • Boosts immunity and aids in stress management
  • Improves wound healing and skin health

Habitat

Arjuna is native to India and grows in riverbanks and dry forests.

Part Used

Bark, leaves, and fruits.

Where It Is Found in India

Arjuna is widely found across India, particularly in Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, and Maharashtra. It grows naturally along riverbanks and in tropical forests.

Preparation Methods

  • Consumed as a decoction for heart health
  • Used in Ayurvedic formulations for cardiovascular support
  • Extracted into herbal tonics and capsules
  • Applied as a paste for skin ailments
  • Boiled in water and consumed as herbal tea
