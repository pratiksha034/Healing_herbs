Shatavari (Asparagus racemosus)

Description: Shatavari is a renowned herb in Ayurveda known for its adaptogenic properties and its benefits for women's reproductive health. It helps balance hormones, improve vitality, and support overall wellness.

Medicinal Uses

  • Supports female reproductive health
  • Enhances fertility and regulates hormonal balance
  • Boosts immunity and overall strength
  • Helps in digestion and reduces acidity
  • Promotes lactation in nursing mothers
  • Has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties

Habitat

Shatavari is native to India and grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in forests and grasslands.

Where It Is Found in India

Shatavari is commonly found in the Himalayan region, central and southern India, including the states of Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Kerala.

Part Used

Roots and leaves.
